Case List

Welcome to Exome Dashboard, an exome analysis simulator. The cases below are based on simulated exomes. In all cases the assumption is that the trio consisting of mother, father, and the proband (the patient) had their exome sequenced, and that of the three, the only person with the described phenotype is the proband.

The goal is to find the mutated gene which is the most likely cause of the reported phenotype.
